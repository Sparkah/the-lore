Judged: Storytelling 40% · Feature completion 30% · Business awareness 30%.

get the product and want to share it. Then publish to the Fotor community and paste
the link on Devpost.

playful. Mirror the app's look.

## Make these 2-3 assets in Fotor

   "POV: the AI in your gc has opinions."
