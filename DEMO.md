# the lore — 3-minute demo script (table-side judging, table 58)

## Before judges arrive
- Laptop on **http://localhost:8787/chat**, server running (`PORT=8787 node server.js`, shows "AI ... LIVE").
- A second tab on **http://localhost:8787/present** (the QR) on a phone-friendly screen, or just have your phone ready.
- The Vibe Hack chat is pre-seeded. Do NOT pre-tap "Add the lore bot" — do it live.
- Have these lines ready to paste (below).

## The 3 minutes

**0:00–0:25 — Problem + user** (don't introduce yourself first)
> "This is a real ZYMIX group chat. Group chats are where Gen Z lives, but they go flat — and social apps struggle to keep people coming back. We turn the chat itself into a game."

**0:25–0:50 — Core idea + add the bot**
> "Meet the lore — a bot you drop into any group chat. It reads the room and scores everyone."
- Tap **Add the lore bot**. It joins, reads the chat, and drops a card per person + an aura leaderboard.

**0:50–2:15 — Live demo (the heart)**
- Let a couple of roasts land (they're funny — let judges read).
> "Everyone gets a character, an aura score and a roast. And it's live — watch."
- Type an **UP** line → tap **update the lore** → aura rises, card refreshes, leaderboard reorders.
- Type a **DOWN (cringe)** line → tap update → aura drops, slides down the board.
- **THE MOMENT — try to game it:** type the gaming line → tap update → it tanks the aura and calls out the manipulation.
> "It has a spine — you can't flatter it or game it."

**2:15–2:40 — AI + workflow**
> "AI isn't a feature here, it IS the product. The reading, the personalities, and the aura rising or falling with consistent values are all live model reasoning — on Claude. Built with Claude Code; the in-product analysis runs on Z.ai's GLM."

**2:40–3:00 — Impact + close**
> "It's a mini-app for a messaging super-app like ZYMIX — an engagement engine that gives a dead group chat a reason to stay alive and pull friends in. And it remembers everything."
- Point at the leaderboard / the aura sparkline. "Scan to try it on your own group chat." (QR on /present.)

## If something breaks
- Slow wifi / AI: the bot falls back to an instant baked reading, so it never stalls — just keep talking over it.
- Let a judge drive: hand them the laptop, have them type a line and tap update.
- Backup demo link for Devpost: record a 2-3 min screen capture of this exact run.
